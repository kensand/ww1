Videos

Footage of WWI is rare, and the footage that exists is in black and white and has no audio. The videos below showcase some of the action that occurred during WWI, some videos have music overlay so sound is not required. One film made during the war, The Battle of the Somme (1916) would be viewed by 20 million, which allowed for people to see the horror of the war. Many movies have been made since the war ended including Lawrence of Arabia(1962) and Wings (1927.)
